Our Mission

Proposed by Christabel Monica Ugbang Agbonkonkon, Black Cyber Recruiter (BCR) is dedicated to connecting blacks and other minorities with job opportunities. BCR simplifies the job search and application process to make finding your dream job easier.
For Applicants: Application deadlines and wage ranges are required for each job listing, and are shown in search results. Other important information such as relocation assistance and if the application requires extra documents, other than your resume, is shown at the top of each job listing.
For Employers: BCR also makes it easy for employers to add, update, and delete job listings, while also making them easy to view, with our simple job listing form.
